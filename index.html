Halifax Regional Coin Club:

During the summer of 2018, I had an oppertunity to work with the Halifax regional coin club to make a website for them to display club info on.

This was an interesting oppertunity and learning expirence for me, as the client wanted to be able to constantly update their web page, but didn't have the budget for a proper content management system. Because of this, after multiple design meetings with the client, I decided to build the website around iframes that refreshed based off a google doc.

I did this project during the beginning of my employment at immediaC, and although I don't think this piece of work is at the same level of quality as some of the projects I worked on at the company, I still feel it was a unique learning expirence for me.

Visit halifaxcoinclub.com
